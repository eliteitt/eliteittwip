Consultations and Workshops

With extensive experience in Interpreting and in Teaching, ELITE ITT specializes in providing consultations and workshops to law firms, government departments and agencies with a need for working with interpreters as well as for cross-cultural interaction.

Consultations and Workshops
  • Vietnamese linguistic and cultural consultations
  • Professional workshops on working with interpreters

Why choose ELITE ITT for Consultations and Workshops on Working with Interpreters?

If you need to consult an expert in the Vietnamese language and culture as well as in cross-cultural interaction for your case, we can be very helpful to you. Book a consultation with us and you will be glad to have done so.

For departments and agencies frequently engaging interpreting services, you and your staff will benefit from and will enjoy our workshops on how to work best with interpreters.
