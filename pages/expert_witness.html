Expert Witness Specialization

One of ELITE ITT’s most highly evaluated specializations is providing Expert Witness reports and evidence in court, particularly for English and Vietnamese Interpreting as well as Translation, in conjunction with our Audio Transcription. We always aim to produce the most competent and reliable transcripts, expert witness reports and evidence while being cost effective.

Based in Sydney, we accept briefs from lawyers and law firms Australia wide and worldwide.

Why choose ELITE ITT to be your Expert Witness?

Having been formally trained in English (Applied Linguistics) including Transcription and Analysis for her MA from University of Wisconsin – Madison, USA, and in Applied Linguistics covering Interpreting and Translation by nature for her PhD from the University of Melbourne, Dr Giao Quynh Tran (Dr Grace Tran) _ Principal of ELITE ITT _ has academic knowledge of the subject matter. Moreover, she has 17 years of frequent practical experience in legal interpreting. She has written elaborate expert witness reports and has appeared a number of times as a defense expert witness in NSW District Court in Sydney.

With research being one of her strengths, Dr Tran has many refereed publications. She received great feedback for her AUSIT Court Interpreting workshop, her article on court interpreting in the Australian Law Society Journal, her presentation on legal interpreting by invitation on Bench TV, and her research article about interpreting in cross-cultural interaction in court published by Elsevier in Oxford, Britain, just to name a few.

In ELITE ITT’s Defense Expert Witness capacity, we provide safeguards for interpreting and translation quality, especially in police interviews and transcripts. Moreover, our qualifications and expertise ensure the strength of our expert witness reports and our expert evidence. Our reports are thoroughly prepared, and our evidence well-grounded, with a view to withstanding cross-examination.
