Why choose ELITE ITT
for all your (Vietnamese and English) Medical Interpreting and Translating needs?

If you are a specialist or a cosmetic surgeon, you may not have access to a public interpreter either onsite or over the phone. It is recommended that you always have a professional interpreter & translator present when your non-English speaking patients sign any document. Besides, patients often complain about the quality of public interpreting services in health. Therefore, ELITE ITT sends our private elite interpreters to hospitals, clinics, private practices, etc. meeting the needs of clients who prefer and trust our services.

Our medical interpreting and translating quality is closely monitored by our Principal Interpreter and Translator _ Dr Giao (Grace) Quynh Tran _ who is a skilled and experienced healthcare interpreter and translator in the U.S and Australia. With her at the forefront, ELITE ITT’s medical interpreting and translating services have exceeded client expectations.

We are here to ensure effective communication between medical practitioners, psychologists, allied health professionals and patients with limited English proficiency. We can help you avoid communication problems which may cost you more than a little down the track.
