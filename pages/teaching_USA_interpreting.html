USA Vietnamese Bilingual Interpreting Examination, Medical Interpreting Test
and Other Assessment Preparation

If you want to become an interpreter and/or a translator in the United States, in most cases you only need to pass the recruiters’ assessment of your interpreting and/or translating skills in the work context you are applying for. All you need is your interpreting and translating skills, which are our expertise, and which are what we teach at ELITE ITT.

ELITE ITT has been highly evaluated for our teaching of Interpreting and Translation in English and Vietnamese, both directions. Being highly experienced in interpreting, especially in court interpreting, and in translating, we are in a position to help you pass the tests you may need to take and become competent English and Vietnamese interpreters and/or translators in your areas of specialization, particularly in legal settings.

With our established online teaching, we are positive about our quality and effective international teaching. Moreover, based in Australia, where English is also the official language, our tuition fees are competitive compared to those where you are.

Our Principal Teacher - Dr Giao Quynh Tran - designs and teaches ELITE ITT’s USA Interpreting and Translation courses, which can be coupled with her automated classes. ELITE ITT’s staff monitors these automated classes to assist with our multi-classroom teaching as necessary.
