TOEFL iBT, IELTS, PTE Academic, OET & CAE Test Training
and English Teaching

ELITE ITT’s forte is teaching English, especially but not limited to Vietnamese speakers. We specialize in training students for the TOEFL iBT, IELTS, PTE Academic, OET and CAE tests.

Our English courses are designed and taught by Dr Giao Quynh Tran and/or our team of experienced native English speaking teachers with at least an MA in English teaching or in a related field. After studying with us, our students have celebrated utterly high Pass rates and marks at the FIRST attempt at their English tests.

To those who are interested in studying our Interpreting and/or Translation courses but are not confident of their language skills, we offer our Foundation Language courses which improve their language proficiency and introduce basic Interpreting as well as Translating. Furthermore, in our program called “Connecting You with Native Speakers around the World”, you will learn English online from our native English speaking teachers with the aforementioned qualifications and many years of experience. Additionally, we offer reasonable tuition fees.
